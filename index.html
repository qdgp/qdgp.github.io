Biography [CV]

I'm going to study as a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Huan Lei at Michigan State University's Computational Mathematics, Science & Engineering Department this fall. I will graduate from the School of Mathematical Science at Soochow University in July 2020, majoring in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics. I am working under the supervision of Prof. Jingrun Chen in Soochow University, focusing on Computational Chemistry and Machine Learning. I took part in the UC Berkeley Extension Program in Spring 2019. During Summer 2019, I participated in a summer research internship at HKUST, advised by Prof. Yang Xiang. I also worked as a visiting student advised by Prof. Zheng Chen on Discontinuous Galerkin Method at The Center for Scientific Computing and Visualization Research at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth in Fall 2019.
